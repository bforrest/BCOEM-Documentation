---
title: "Seeing Judge Session Sign-ups"
---

**Q:** How do I see what judging sessions judges have signed up for before they are sorted to tables?

**A:** To see which locations/sessions your judges have indicated that they're available for, go to Admin Dashboard > Entries and Participants > Assign Judges. 

Their sessions are listed in the Preferences column - use the search feature to narrow down by session name. Or, you can download a CSV by going to Admin Dashboard > Data Exports > Email Addresses > Available Judges.
